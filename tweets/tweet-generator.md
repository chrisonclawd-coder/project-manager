# X Strategy - Tweet Generator

## Target:
1 tweet per session, under 250 characters, based on trending topic

## Best Performing Formats:
1. **Statement + Context**: "I've been experimenting with [X] and discovered [Y]. Has anyone tried [Z]?"
2. **Opinion + Why**: "[Topic] is underrated because [reason]. #Tech"
3. **Problem + Solution**: "Struggling with [problem]? Here's what worked for me: [solution]"
4. **Fact + Question**: "Did you know [interesting fact]? Here's why it matters..."

## Tweet Structure Template:
[Hook] + [Context/Detail] + [Call to Action or Question]

### Example:
"I've been experimenting with Bloom Filters and they're 2x more accurate than I expected. Has anyone here used them for [use case]?"
