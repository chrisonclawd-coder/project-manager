# X Algorithm Strategy - Plain English Plan

## What X's Algorithm Actually Does

**It learns from your actions.**

1. **It watches you:** What you like, reply to, repost, click, share
2. **It finds similar content:** Through Phoenix (ML discovery)
3. **It predicts what you'll engage with:** Using Grok transformer
4. **It ranks posts:** Higher engagement = higher score = appears in your feed
5. **It filters:** Removes duplicates, old posts, blocked accounts, spam

**The key insight:** The algorithm has **zero hand-engineered rules**. No magic hashtag formulas. No follower count filters. It just predicts what you'll engage with based on your history.

## The 7-Day Training Plan

### **Day 1-3: Teach the Algorithm What You Like**

**Goal:** Build engagement history so the algorithm knows what types of content you engage with.

#### **Daily Routine (Every Day)**

**Morning (9am-11am IST):**
- Engage with 10 posts from your feed
- Reply to 3 posts from accounts you follow
- Like 5-10 posts
- Do NOT repost yet

**Afternoon (2pm-4pm IST):**
- Post 1 tweet
- Engage with 5 more posts

**Evening (8pm-10pm IST):**
- Reply to 3 more posts
- Engage with 5 more posts

#### **Content Strategy (Day 1-3)**

**Post 1 tweet per day:**
- Hook: Under 80 characters
- Body: 3-4 sentences
- CTA: Ask a question or tag someone
- Format: Image + text (or just text)
- Mention 1 tech creator (@vaalak07, @othercreator, etc.)

**Example tweet structure:**
```
Hook: "This broke my brain about [topic]"

Body: [3-4 sentences explaining why it matters]

CTA: "Tag someone who needs to see this: @mention"

Format: [Image/text/thread/link]
```

**What to engage with:**
- Posts from tech accounts you follow
- Posts about AI, tools, productivity
- Posts that are controversial or opinionated (they trigger replies)
- Posts from accounts with 1K-100K followers (easier to get engagement)

**What to avoid:**
- Followed accounts that never post (waste of time)
- Self-promotion posts
- Viral content that has nothing to do with your interests

---

### **Day 4-6: Test and Optimize**

**Goal:** See what works and double down.

#### **Measurements (Every Day)**

Track:
- Views per post
- Likes per post
- Replies per post
- Reposts per post

**What to look for:**
- Which hooks get more engagement?
- Which posting times work best?
- Which types of content get more replies?
- Which tech creators' posts get more engagement?

#### **Content Strategy (Day 4-6)**

**Based on Day 1-3 data:**

**If replies are high:**
- Post more thread format
- Ask questions at the end
- Include controversial statements
- Start threads with hooks

**If likes are high:**
- Use more images
- Keep posts scannable
- Use hooks, numbers, questions, claims
- 2-3 emojis max

**If reposts are high:**
- Make content shareable
- Include "Repost if you agree" CTAs
- Use proven formats (top 3 lists, how-to guides, controversial takes)
- Mention other tech creators

**Post frequency:** Still 1x per day at optimal time

---

### **Day 7-14: Aggressive Growth**

**Goal:** Get the algorithm to show your posts to more people.

#### **Content Strategy (Day 7-14)**

**Double down on what works:**
- If Day 4-6 data shows 150+ views per post, keep that format
- If Day 4-6 data shows 2+ likes per post, keep that format
- If Day 4-6 data shows 1+ reply per post, keep that format

**Post frequency:** 1-2x per day at optimal times

**Optimal posting times (test these):**
- 12pm IST (lunch, tech workers online)
- 3pm IST (afternoon work break)
- 6pm IST (evening commute)
- 9pm IST (after dinner, peak activity)

**Engagement frequency:**
- Reply to 3-5 posts per day
- Repost 1-2 posts that interest you
- Like 10-15 posts per day
- Mention 2-3 tech creators per day

**Mention strategy:**
- Mention tech creators who post about topics you like
- They often engage back with their followers
- Cross-pollination: their audience sees you, you see them
- Don't just mention anyone - mention creators with similar follower count (1K-100K) or bigger

## What the Algorithm Actually Needs From You

### **1. Engagement History (Days 1-7)**
- Like, reply, repost different types of content
- This teaches the algorithm what you like
- Phoenix uses this to find similar content

### **2. Diverse Engagement (Days 1-14)**
- Don't just like
- Reply to trigger algorithm attention
- Repost to show you share content
- Click on media to trigger media engagement predictions

### **3. Consistent Posting (Days 1-14)**
- The algorithm needs patterns
- 1-2x per day is better than sporadic posting
- Consistency helps Phoenix find your content patterns

### **4. Multiple Engagement Types (Days 4-14)**
- Posts that trigger like + reply + repost
- These have the highest weighted scores
- Your post gets pushed higher in the feed

### **5. Quality Content (Every Day)**
- High engagement prediction score
- Scannable, clear, actionable
- Includes hooks, CTAs, media
- Mentioning other creators

**Why this is okay:** The algorithm is still learning. Engagement is building.

- Reposts: 1-3 per post

**Why this matters:** Algorithm is starting to understand your preferences.

### **Week 3+ (Days 15+):**

- Likes: 5-10 per post

- Reposts: 3-5 per post

**Why this is big:** Your content is now in the "high engagement" bucket. Phoenix shows your content to more people.

## What If It Doesn't Work?

**If Week 2 targets aren't met:**

**Week 3 pivot:**
- Test different hooks (bold statements vs questions)
- Test different formats (image vs text vs thread)
- Test different posting times (every hour to find peak)
- Test different tech creators (mention more, less, different niches)

**If still no engagement:**
- Re-evaluate your topic selection
- Are you posting about tech? Are you genuinely interested?
- Algorithm doesn't reward fake engagement
- If you don't actually care about what you're posting, the algorithm will figure it out

**If you're getting blocked/muted:**
- Stop posting controversial content
- Focus on helpful, educational content
- Engage respectfully with others

## The Bottom Line

**X's algorithm is simple:**
1. It watches what you do
2. It learns your preferences
3. It shows you content it thinks you'll engage with
4. It shows your content to people it thinks will engage with it

**Your job:**
1. Teach it what you like (engage with 10-15 posts/day)
2. Show consistent posting (1-2x/day)
3. Make content that triggers engagement (hooks, CTAs, media, mentions)

5. Test, measure, optimize (7 days minimum)

**The algorithm doesn't care about follower count, hashtags, or follower count. It only cares about engagement.**

**Your strategy: build engagement → algorithm learns → algorithm shows your content to more people.**
